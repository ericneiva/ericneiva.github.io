--- #### Shape emergence via mechanochemical instabilities The interplay of molecule transport and surface-bulk hydrodynamics is characterised by two adimensional parameters: $$ \mathrm{Pe} = \frac{\xi_{\Gamma,0} R^2}{2 \mu_\Gamma D_\Gamma} = \frac{\text{contractile forces}}{\text{diffusive forces}} \quad \text{and} \quad \frac{\mu_\Gamma / \mu_\Omega}{R} \gg 1 $$ A perturbation of homogeneous $m_\Gamma$ leads to pattern formation. <div class="footnote"> * Mietke et al., PRL 123(18):188101 (2019) * Wittwer and Aland., J Comp Phys X 17:100126 (2023) </div>
